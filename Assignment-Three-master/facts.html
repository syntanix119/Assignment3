Did You Know?

  1. 94% of life on Earth is aquatic.
  2. About two-thirds of all marine life remains unidentified.
  3. There are actually lakes and rivers that exist underwater in the ocean.
  4. About 70% of the oxygen that we breathe comes from the ocean.
  5. The largest living structure in the world is The Great Barrier Reef.
  6. The world's oceans contain approximately 20 million tons of gold.
  7. Over 90% of all volcanic activity happens in the ocean.
